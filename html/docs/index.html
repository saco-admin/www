External links

  • Other olympiads and training facilities
    • IOI website. This page has all past IOI problems, as well as links to many national and regional olympiads.
    • The USA training pages, with on-line evaluation. A very good place to start training.
    • TopCoder: regular online contests with a rating system. Unfortunately most of the contests are in the early hours of the morning on South African time, but all the past contests are available in the practice area.
    • UVa online judge. Has thousands of problems with on-line evaluation.
    • ACM ICPC, the big international university contest.
    • IPSC, an online contest for teams of three open to anyone.
